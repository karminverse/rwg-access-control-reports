About

What every report establishes

Which levers can change an asset, who can pull them, and what stands in the way.

Each report identifies which addresses can mint, upgrade, pause, blacklist, or re-price the asset, which critical parameter levers (mint caps, debt ceilings, fee rates, oracle pointers) they control, and whether that authority sits behind a multisig and a timelock or a single key. Holders are followed recursively to terminal Gnosis Safes and timelocks, parameter changes are tracked with their history, and every address is screened against sanctions lists.

Findings are observational and are a rigorous starting point for analyst review, not a substitute for a full audit. Reports are generated by Trustfall and curated by the Inverse Finance Risk Working Group.
